How to Create Your Personal AI Profile in 30 Minutes

January 15, 2025 • 8 min read

After spending countless hours working with AI, I've learned one fundamental truth: without context about who you are and how you work, AI is essentially useless. It's like sending that genius old man to the grocery store without telling him what you need—you'll get Diet Pepsi when you asked for milk.

The single most impactful thing you can do to make AI useful? Create a comprehensive "About Me" profile. Here's exactly how to do it in 30 minutes.

Why Context Changes Everything

Large Language Models are, at their core, guessing agents. They predict the next word based on all the words that came before. When you only give them a single line of instruction like "rewrite this email," they can go in countless directions. But when you provide rich context about who you are, how you communicate, and what you value, suddenly their guesses become remarkably accurate.

The 30-Minute Profile Framework

Set a timer and work through these sections:

  • Professional Identity (5 minutes): Your role, company, industry, years of experience, and areas of expertise.
  • Communication Style (10 minutes): Are you direct or diplomatic? Formal or casual? Do you use analogies? Prefer bullet points or paragraphs? Love or hate emojis?
  • Values and Principles (5 minutes): What drives your decisions? What won't you compromise on? What's your approach to problem-solving?
  • Specific Preferences (5 minutes): Pet peeves, favorite phrases, things you always include or never say.
  • Context and Goals (5 minutes): What are you trying to achieve? Who's your audience? What challenges do you face regularly?
EXAMPLE PROFILE START: I am Brooks Swift, CEO of SmackDab. I've been in business for 20 years, starting with home automation companies and moving into software. I'm very direct in communication, avoid fluff, and get straight to the point. I use bullet points for clarity and rarely use emojis. I believe in human-first technology and that AI should amplify human ability, not replace it...

Using Your Profile Effectively

Once you have your profile, here's how to leverage it:

  • Create Project Templates: In Claude, create different projects for different contexts (personal emails, business proposals, technical documentation).
  • Iterate and Improve: After each interaction, ask the AI "What did you learn about me from this conversation?" and add those insights to your profile.
  • Keep It Fresh: Update your profile monthly as your role, goals, or communication needs evolve.
Pro Tip: Don't just create one profile. Create specific profiles for different contexts. Your "replying to client emails" profile should be different from your "internal team communication" profile.

Remember: AI without context is just sophisticated guessing. But with the right context—your context—it becomes a powerful amplifier of your abilities. Spend the 30 minutes. Create your profile. Watch your AI interactions transform from frustrating to actually useful.
